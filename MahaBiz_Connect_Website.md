# MahaBiz Connect Website - AI IDE Development Prompt

## Project Overview

Build a modern, premium, responsive React website for **MahaBiz Connect**, Maharashtra's Business Growth Ecosystem.

Tagline:

> CONNECT • PROMOTE • GROW

The platform is NOT a traditional business directory.

It should be positioned as a business growth ecosystem helping:

- Startups
- MSMEs
- Small Businesses
- Retailers
- Manufacturers
- Service Providers
- Professionals
- Consultants
- Industry Associations

The primary goals are:

- Generate Membership Registrations
- Generate Business Leads
- Promote Featured Businesses
- Showcase Services
- Build Business Community
- Host Events
- Sell Membership Plans
- Capture Enquiries
- Publish Success Stories
- Build MahaBiz Connect Brand
